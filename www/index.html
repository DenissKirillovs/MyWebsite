About Me

Hi! I'm Deniss Kirillovs, a game visual scripter and a designer who originated from Latvia. I am currently studying at Bournemouth University and I am very passionate and a commited indivudal who excels at visual scripting in Unreal Engine 4, map and environmental design.

I have also created and worked on numerous game ready models using 3DS MAX and Maya - which are both tools that I am comfortable in using.
